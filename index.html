A LITTLE ABOUT US

Trakomatic enables businesses and organizations to discover the ways people interact in a physical environment using video and sensor technology.

We capture profile and behavior data and transform it into actionable insights that drives optimization and improvement of your core business processes.

  • Demographic analysis based on facial characteristics
  • Age group, gender and ethnicity classification
  • Attention time: time spent in front of camera
  • New vs. repeat visitor detection

  • Overhead intelligent sensors
  • Omnidirectional people counting
  • Walk-in to walk-by ratio
  • Premises occupancy
